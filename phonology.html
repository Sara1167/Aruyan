1. In words of Aruyan origin د is predominantly used. While in the beginning of the syllable ذ ض can also appear, they cannot appear at the end unless the word is of Arabic origin.

2. The letter is pronounced as /d/ at the end of the syllable or the beginning of the word, but in other cases as /ð/

3. In words of Aruyan origin only ه is used

4. In words of Aruyan origin in the beginning of the syllable only ك can be used

5. Both ك خ are pronounced as /k/ in the beginning of the syllable, but can be pronounced as /k/ or /x/ at the end of it

6. ‎In the words of Aruyan origin ‎ز is only used at the end of the syllable, ث is used only for negation, ‎ص is used for a long /s/ sound and س is used at the beginning of the syllable.

7. In words of Aruyan origin ط is rarely used, the usually used form is ت

8. For both letters versions with ء are used on the end of the syllable, while versions without at the beginning

9. Words of Aruyan origin use only ع while ء is only used in Arabic loanwords
